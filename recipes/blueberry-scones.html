Glazed Lemon Blueberry Scones

Description

Bright, zesty, and incredibly tender, these café-style scones are a total showstopper. This foolproof recipe relies on cold butter and heavy cream to create flaky, melt-in-your-mouth layers. Bursting with juicy blueberries and finished with a sweet lemon glaze, they make the perfect weekend breakfast or afternoon tea treat.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • ⅓ cup granulated white sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 21 tbsp lemon zest (freshly grated)
  • ½ cup (1 stick) unsalted butter, frozen
  • ½ cup heavy cream (plus 2 tbsp for brushing)
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 cup fresh blueberries
  • Glaze: 1 cup powdered sugar + 2 tbsp fresh lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Whisk flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, and lemon zest in a large bowl. Use a box grater to grate the frozen butter directly into the flour. Toss gently with a fork to coat the butter pieces.
  2. In a separate measuring cup, whisk the heavy cream and egg. Pour into the flour mixture, add the blueberries, and fold gently until a shaggy dough starts to form.
  3. Turn the dough onto a floured surface and gently pat it into an 8-inch disc. Use a sharp knife to cut the disc into 8 equal wedges (like a pizza). Place wedges on a lined baking sheet and brush tops with a bit of heavy cream.
  4. Bake until the edges are golden brown. Let them cool completely. Whisk the powdered sugar and lemon juice together, then drizzle generously over the cooled scones.
