One-Bowl Fudgy Chocolate Chip Brownies

Description

Say goodbye to box mixes forever. This effortless, one-bowl recipe yields the ultimate fudgy brownie: ultra-dense, intensely chocolatey, and topped with that coveted, paper-thin crinkle crust. Packed with chocolate chips for a double dose of decadence, these brownies are thick, chewy, and take less than 10 minutes to prep.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup (1 stick) unsalted butter, melted and warm
  • 1 tbsp cooking oil (vegetable or canola)
  • 1 cup granulated white sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • ½ cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ¾ cup semi-sweet chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven and line an 8x8 inch baking pan with parchment paper, leaving an overhang on the sides for easy removal later.
  2. In a medium bowl, vigorously whisk the melted butter, oil, and sugar together for about a minute. Add the eggs and vanilla, whisking hard until the mixture becomes slightly pale.
  3. Sift the flour, cocoa powder, and salt directly into the bowl. Switch to a spatula and fold just until combined.
  4. Gently fold in the chocolate chips. Spread the thick batter evenly into your prepared pan. Bake for 20 to 22 minutes—the center should still be slightly soft for maximum fudginess.
